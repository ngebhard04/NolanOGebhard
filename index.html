What is Algorithmic Bias?

Algorithmic Bias appears when systematic errors occur in machine learning algorithms that produce unfair or discriminary outcomes. It often reflects or reinforces existing socioeconoic, racial, and gender biases (Jonker, Rogers).

Algorithmic Bias can influence decisions in topics like healthcare and employment, and can lead to inequality to certain groups or people around the world. Algorithmic Bias can be in a lot more than just hiring systems, as it can be implimented into just about any system that follows structured rules.
